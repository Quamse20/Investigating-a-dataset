Introduction¶

In this project i will be analyzing data associated with patients not showing up on their scheduled date of appointment based on some independent variables

This dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether or not patients show up for their appointment. A number of characteristics about the patient are included in each row. ScheduledDay tells us on what day the patient set up their appointment. Neighborhood indicates the location of the hospital. 'Gender' indicates the sex of the patients 'Age' indicates the age of the patients 'SMS-received' indicates whether or not a patients received a scholarship or not Scholarship indicates whether or not the patient is enrolled in Brasilian welfare program Bolsa Famlia. 'No-show': indicates whether a patient shows for an appointment or not; it says No if the patient showed up to their appointment, and Yes if they did not show up.

In particular, i am interested in finding the trends among the patients who showed up for their appointments, and how they differed from the patients who didn't show up.

QUESTION WE CAN ASK ABOUT THE DATA SET¶

(1) Is there an association between gender and the patient showing for appointments or not? (i.e. does being a male or female have any effect on patients showing for their appointments

(2) Is there a relationship between age with the patient showing or not for appointment? (i.e. does being in certain age group (e.g. youths, adults, middle age, and old adults) have any effect on patients showing for their analysis.

(3) Is there a relationship between sms received with the patient showing or not for appointment? (i.e. does sms_received by the patients, reminding them for their appointments have any effect of patients showing up for their analysis).

(4) Is there a relationship between scholarship with the patient showing or not for appointment? (i.e. does awarding a patients scholarship have any effect of patients showing up for their analysis).

The Independent variables¶

gender- Does being a male or female affect appointments of a patient¶
age- Does ageGroup affect appointments of a patient¶
scholarship- Does giving a patient scholarship or not affect the patients from showing for their appointments¶
sms_received- Does sending a patient sms for a reminder for their appointment or not affect their appointment¶

The Dependent variable¶

No_show- (0-showing for appointments, 1-not showing for appointment)¶
We find the relationship between our independent variables and dependent variable using statistics and visualizations¶
